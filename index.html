Hi, my name is Marco Del Prato, Welcome!

I'm a 18 y.o music student and at the moment I'm attending the second year of university in the 'Istituto Superiore di Studi Musicali Achille Peri-Claudio Merulo' in the city of Reggio Emilia (IT).
I really love baroque music and my favourite composer of all time is J.S Bach.
In my free time I have lots of other passions too, like painting and philosophy.
In this small website you can find my Contacts, my Curriculum Vitae, my small Youtube Channel , where I post pieces of my concerts and some music I record myself playing, and also a page where you can donate some money in order for me to be able to continue and finance my future studies and projects.
Of course you can also request any piece of music to be played, I would be happy to perform it and share it with you!
Have a nice one!
